1. Services

Opini Visual ("we", "us") provides white-label video editing services to wedding studios and filmmakers ("client", "you"), including but not limited to feature film edits, highlight reels, social media reels, and same-day edits, as described on our Services and Pricing pages.

2. Project Scope & Onboarding

Each project begins with a brief style guide and reference materials provided by the client. Work begins once footage and the agreed deposit (if applicable) have been received. Changes to scope after work has begun may affect price and delivery date.

3. Payment

Pricing for each package is listed on our Pricing page and confirmed in writing before work begins. Payment terms (deposit and balance, or full payment upfront) will be agreed upon per project or per ongoing arrangement. Final files are released upon full payment.

4. Revisions

Each package includes a set number of revision rounds as described on our Pricing page. Additional revisions beyond what's included may be billed at an hourly rate, agreed upon before the work is carried out.

5. Turnaround & Delays

Estimated turnaround times are listed on our Workflow page. Delays caused by late footage delivery, incomplete briefs, or delayed client feedback may extend the delivery date accordingly. We will communicate any expected delay as soon as we're aware of it.

6. Ownership & Usage Rights

All raw footage remains the property of the client or the client's studio. Once a project is paid in full, the client owns the final delivered edit and may use it without restriction, including delivering it to their own clients under their own brand.

7. White-Label Confidentiality

We operate strictly as a white-label partner. Opini Visual will not disclose that we edited a specific project without the client's written permission, and we do not add our own branding to delivered files.

8. Portfolio Use

We do not feature any client's footage or delivered edits in our own portfolio or marketing without explicit written permission from the client.

9. Cancellations

Projects cancelled before work has begun may be eligible for a partial refund of any deposit paid, minus any administrative costs already incurred. Projects cancelled after editing has started are billed for work completed to date.

10. Limitation of Liability

Opini Visual is not liable for loss or damage to footage during transfer through third-party platforms (Dropbox, Google Drive, Frame.io, or similar), nor for delays caused by circumstances outside our reasonable control. We recommend clients retain their own backup of all raw footage.

11. Changes to These Terms

We may update these terms from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
