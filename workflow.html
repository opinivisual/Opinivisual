Workflow

A process built to disappear into yours

You won't need to change your software, your delivery format, or how you brief a project. Opini Visual adapts to your studio — not the other way around.

Step 1

Upload Footage

Send raw footage through Dropbox, Google Drive, or Frame.io — whichever your studio already relies on. Along with the footage, we ask for your style guide once during onboarding, then reuse it for every project after.

Step 2

We Cut

Your dedicated editor works in DaVinci Resolve or Premiere Pro — whichever you prefer — and builds the story around your established pacing, music selection, and color signature. No generic template edits.

Step 3

Review & Revise

You review the cut through Frame.io, leaving timestamped comments directly on the timeline. We revise until it matches what you'd have delivered yourself — two rounds included on every package.

Step 4

Delivery

Final export delivered in every format and resolution your client package requires — from full-resolution masters to platform-ready social cuts — uploaded straight to your delivery folder.
