Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website, book a call, or become a client, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and studio/business name
  • Messages, project briefs, and files you send us (including raw wedding footage)
  • Basic usage data such as pages visited and general location, collected via standard analytics tools

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule calls
  • Deliver editing services you've requested, including reviewing and returning your footage
  • Send project updates and, occasionally, relevant service information
  • Improve our website and services

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your information. We may share information with trusted third-party tools we rely on to run our business — such as file storage (Dropbox, Google Drive, Frame.io), form processing (Formspree), and communication tools — solely to deliver the services you've requested.

4. Footage and Project Files

Raw footage and project files sent to us remain your property. We do not use client footage for promotional purposes — including our portfolio — without your explicit written permission. Files are retained only as long as needed to complete revisions, then deleted according to our standard retention schedule unless otherwise agreed.

5. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to remember your preferences and understand how visitors use the site. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information and footage from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us at hello@opinivisual.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices. The "last updated" date above will always reflect the most recent revision.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@opinivisual.com.
