Services

Every format your studio delivers, cut by one dedicated team

Service 01

Feature Films

Full-length cinematic wedding films, typically 30 minutes to 1 hour, cut with the pacing of a documentary rather than a highlight reel. Every ceremony beat, speech, and reception moment placed with intention.

  • Full ceremony and reception coverage
  • Custom sound design and music licensing guidance
  • Color grading matched to your studio's signature look
  • Two rounds of revision included

Service 02

Highlight Reels

3–6 minute cuts built for sharing — the version couples send to everyone who couldn't make the wedding. Paced for social platforms without losing the emotional arc of the day.

  • Optimized for Instagram, YouTube, and client social sharing
  • Licensed music selection from your approved library
  • Vertical and horizontal exports included
  • Typical delivery: 5–7 business days
