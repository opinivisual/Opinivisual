About Opini Visual

Built by an editor who got tired of watching studios turn down bookings

Opini Visual didn't start as an agency. It started as one editor, one client, and a deadline that felt impossible — the same problem every growing studio runs into.

The Beginning

Every studio hits the same wall

A studio gets good. Bookings pick up. And then the founder — the same person shooting weekends and grading footage until 2am — realizes the bottleneck was never talent or demand. It was hours in the day.

Opini Visual was built to remove that bottleneck without asking studios to hire, train, or manage anyone. We plug into your existing workflow — your style guide, your software, your delivery standards — as a dedicated editing team that happens to work off-site.

What We Hold To

The standards behind every delivery

Consistency Over Flash

Your clients should never be able to tell which wedding was cut by which editor. Style guides are followed exactly, every time.

Direct Communication

No account managers relaying messages. You talk to the editor working on your film, through the tools you already use.

Deadlines Are Promises

A delivery date isn't an estimate. If we say Friday, your client gets their film Friday.
